Resource Profile: openIMIS Contract

Defining URL:https://openimis.github.io/openimis_fhir_r4_ig/StructureDefinition/openimis-contract
Version:0.1.0
Name:OpenIMISContract
Title:openIMIS Contract
Status:Active as of 2021-10-04T15:42:19+00:00
Definition:

Defines a Contract for openIMIS use to enrol a Family/Group. Mapped mostly to Policy and will link Group/Family and Patient/Insurees.

Publisher:Swiss TPH
Source Resource:XML / JSON / Turtle

The official URL for this profile is: 

https://openimis.github.io/openimis_fhir_r4_ig/StructureDefinition/openimis-contract
