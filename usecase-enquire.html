Enquire Use Case

In this use case, a Point of Service (PoS) is enquiring for the Insuree’s policy details.

The following resources are involved in this workflow:

  • ActivityDefinition for the reference to the medical service
  • Medication for the reference to the medical item
  • coverageEligibilityRequest to post information about the enquire
  • coverageEligibilityResponse to get the response about the enquire
